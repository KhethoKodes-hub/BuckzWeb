Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a basic car wash take? +
Our basic car wash typically takes 15-20 minutes. However, during peak hours there might be a short wait. We recommend visiting during weekdays for quicker service.
Do I need to make an appointment? +
No appointment is necessary for our standard services! We operate on a first-come, first-served basis. For larger vehicles or multiple vehicles, we recommend calling ahead.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept cash, card payments, and mobile money transfers. Corporate accounts are also available for regular customers.
Can I wait while my car is being washed? +
Absolutely! We have a comfortable waiting area where you can relax. Soon we'll also be offering refreshments and food services while you wait.
Do you offer services for larger vehicles? +
Yes, we can accommodate SUVs, bakkies, and small commercial vehicles. For very large vehicles, please contact us in advance to discuss your specific needs.
