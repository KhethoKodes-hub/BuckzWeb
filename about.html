Our Story

Buckz One Stop (Pty) Ltd was formalized by Saartjie Mmenyane Selatwe in 2023 after operating an informal car wash in Sesheng, Kathu town for a number of years. Our strategic location near the mine makes us easily accessible to passing motorists in one of the busiest areas of Kathu.

What started as a small informal operation has now grown into a trusted business serving the Kathu community with premium car wash services. Our journey reflects our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.
